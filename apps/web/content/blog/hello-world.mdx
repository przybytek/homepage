Welcome to my corner of the internet. This site is my personal space to share thoughts on software
engineering, cloud infrastructure, and whatever else is occupying my brain at the moment.

## What to expect

- **Deep dives** into AWS, CDK, and infrastructure-as-code
- **Project write-ups** — how I built something and what I learned
- **Short takes** on tools, patterns, and things I find interesting

## Built with

This blog is powered by [Next.js 15](https://nextjs.org) with MDX posts stored in-repo —
no headless CMS, no database. Just markdown files that get rendered server-side.

The full source is on [GitHub](https://github.com/przybo/homepage).

Stay tuned.
